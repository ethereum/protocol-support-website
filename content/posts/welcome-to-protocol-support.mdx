We're excited to launch the Protocol Support website, a new resource for anyone interested in understanding and contributing to Ethereum protocol development.

## What is Protocol Support?

Protocol Support is an Ethereum Foundation team dedicated to facilitating the Ethereum protocol development process. Like our octopus mascot with its many arms, we reach into different areas of the ecosystem:

- **AllCoreDevs Coordination** - We help organize the bi-weekly calls where protocol changes are discussed
- **PM Repository Management** - We maintain the central hub for meeting notes and scheduling
- **Contributor Onboarding** - We help newcomers navigate the complex landscape of protocol governance
- **Cross-team Communication** - We bridge different stakeholder groups across the ecosystem

## Why This Website?

The ethereum/pm repository is a treasure trove of information, but it can be intimidating for newcomers. This website aims to be a friendly entry point that helps you:

1. Understand how Ethereum governance works
2. Find the resources you need
3. Discover ways to contribute
4. Stay updated on protocol development

## Get Involved

Whether you're a seasoned protocol developer or just curious about how Ethereum evolves, there's a place for you:

- **Learn** - Explore our [Resources](/resources) page for curated learning materials
- **Contribute** - Check out our [Contributing](/contributing) guide to find your path
- **Participate** - Watch AllCoreDevs calls or join [breakout discussions](/pm-repo/breakouts)
